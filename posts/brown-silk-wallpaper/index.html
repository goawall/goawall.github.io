How is wallpaper made? X Wallpaper is a popular choice for many homeowners looking to add a personal touch to their décor. But how is wallpaper made? Wallpaper is created by first printing the design onto paper. Once the design is printed, it is then coated with a thin layer of vinyl. The vinyl helps to protect the wallpaper from moisture and also makes it more durable.

After the vinyl coating is applied, the wallpaper is then cut into strips that are about 12 inches wide. These strips are then pasted onto the wall using a special adhesive.
