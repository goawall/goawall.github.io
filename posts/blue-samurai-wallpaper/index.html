The history of minimalist wallpaper Wallpaper has been around for centuries, but it wasn’t until the late 20th century that minimalist wallpaper became popular. In the world of wallpaper, this means designs that are free of busy patterns and unnecessary embellishments. This new style was a reaction against the excesses of previous decades, and minimalism’s emphasis on simplicity and functionality resonated with many people.
